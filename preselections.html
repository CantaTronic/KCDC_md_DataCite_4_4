Important Note:

Since a 'Full Data Set' including the data arrays ('e/γ-deposits', 'μ-deposits' and 'arrival times') for KASCADE and COMBINED are much too large for download (> 500 GB), most of the data sets we offer are data samples without the data arrays on the 'Preselections' download page.

If however you are interesteed in the full KCDC data sample you have to request it in portions by applying appropriate cuts for example on 'Run Number' (R) or on 'Global Time' (GT) because the file space per request is limited to 50GB to keep it manageable.

The 'small data samples' are provided to help you to find the most effective cuts to submit a request as efficiently as possible. Here data sets with and without data arrays are available.

If you need any help to get the data sample you would like to have, please do not hesitate to contact us    ' ' .

Data Preselections
This menu offers the option to download preselected data samples for the detectors 'KASCADE' and 'COMBINED' directly without selecting quantities and applying cuts in the DataShops.
'details' provides a more detailed information poge of the respective data set.
The 'Small Data Samples' offer the opportunity to check your own requirements on a samall data smple statistically evenly distributed.
The 'download' button connects to our download server.
